Welcome to the First Grade Preference Analyzer

About the Project

At Nova Horizons Academy, we believe in understanding and nurturing the unique preferences and dynamics of our first-grade students. Our web application, the First Grade Preference Analyzer, is designed to provide valuable insights into the social interactions and academic preferences of our young learners.

Features

  • Questionnaire Page: Students can submit their preferences, including their favorite course and preferred play/work partners.
  • Summary Page: Gain insights into student pairs with mutual preferences, top 3 most popular students within each class, and a list of courses sorted by popularity.
